### Plain-English read of the flags

- **Em dashes (55).** Tucker Max uses dashes as default beat-punctuation throughout — they're a stylistic fingerprint of his prose, not a sign of generation. The pattern still trips the check because em-dash density is the single highest-volume 2026 AI tell. Genuine voice device, but the count is well above any "purposeful" threshold.
- **"And honestly" (2×).** In confessional memoir this reads as a colloquial intensifier, not insight-laundering. The grader can't tell the difference between "and honestly, I think I failed at meditation" (voice) and "and honestly, this reveals something deeper" (autopilot). Likely a false positive in this register.
- **Contrived contrast (4×).** Look for the not-X-but-Y constructions in the draft (e.g., "Not as recreational drugs. *They're into them as therapies and medicines*"). Some are genuinely contrived; others are voice-driven setups that pay off.
- **Section scaffolding.** The "[" repetition is from Medium's footer link markers (Mental Health / Mdma / Therapy tags) — an artefact of the source export, not the prose itself.
- **23 headings.** This is a Medium long-form article packaged with a numbered table-of-contents structure ("1. Why I Decided To Do MDMA", "2. How I Found My MDMA Therapist", through "9. MDMA Reading & Resources"). For an 8,000-word essay it's defensible structure; for shorter prose it would read as scaffolding.
- **301 curly quotes.** Almost all are apostrophes (don't, can't, I'm, it's) auto-curled by Medium's renderer. Not a writer choice.
- **Anaphora ("you" × 5).** Direct address to the reader — confessional/instructional register. Voice device.
- **"Profound."** Tucker uses it twice in the opener to set stakes. Stock hype word, but characteristic of his confessional-bro voice.
- **Triads (27, density flag).** Confessional rhythm leans heavily on three-beat phrasing here. Some are voice ("trust the medicine, surrender to the medicine, receive what comes up" — quoted from a book title, can't be removed). Others are autopilot. Density is genuinely high.
- **Vocabulary diversity (TTR 0.180).** Long pieces naturally drop in TTR — 7,977 words is past the point where 0.40 is achievable for any human writer. The threshold is calibrated for shorter prose; this is a length artefact more than a style artefact.
