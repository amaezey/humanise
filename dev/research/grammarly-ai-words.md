## Additional AI Text Characteristics Noted (not specific words)

The article also identifies these structural patterns of AI writing:
- Formal, robotic tone
- Repetitive phrasing
- Lack of personal touch / personality / creativity
- Predictable formatting (title-case headings, bullet points, polished phrasing)
- LLMs "generate text by predicting the most likely next word based on patterns"
