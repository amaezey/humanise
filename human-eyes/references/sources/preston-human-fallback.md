## Summary
Literary/cultural essay about Preston's work as a human fallback operator for a real-estate conversational AI called Brenda. The saved article describes human operators editing or intervening when the bot could not handle out-of-script replies, missing specifics, ambiguity, or emotionally complex cases.

## Evidence and claims to extract
- Brenda scanned incoming messages for keywords, assigned classification tags, and queued boilerplate responses for a human operator to review before a timer elapsed.
- Brenda struggled with wrong classifications, missing specifics, out-of-script replies, and emotionally complex disclosures.
- HUMAN_FALLBACK occurred when prospects' responses did not fit a simple Yes/No script or required human contextual judgement.
- The source gives process examples: when it came to specifics, neither Brenda nor the operators could say much; Brenda was positive and competent but not allowed to say "I don't know."
