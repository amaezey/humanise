## Summary
Nature news coverage of Kobak et al.'s biomedical-abstract analysis. The article reports that around one in seven biomedical-research abstracts published in 2024 were probably written with AI help, and that more than 200,000 of 1.5 million PubMed-indexed 2024 abstracts contained words commonly suggested by LLMs.
