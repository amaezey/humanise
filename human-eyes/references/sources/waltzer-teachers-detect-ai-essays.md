## Summary
Empirical study testing whether high school teachers and students can distinguish high school student essays from ChatGPT-generated essays. Participants read essay pairs and guessed which one was generated by ChatGPT.

## Main insights
- Human detection was only moderately successful: teachers reached about 70% accuracy and students about 62%.
- Confidence, ChatGPT experience, and subject-matter expertise did not predict accuracy in the abstracted result.
- Well-written student essays were especially hard to distinguish from ChatGPT texts.

## Evidence and claims to extract
- Participants: 69 high school teachers and 140 high school students, total N=209.
- Task: AI Identification Test with paired essays, one high school student-written and one ChatGPT-generated.
