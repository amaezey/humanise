## Evidence and claims to extract
- Futurism reports that Sports Illustrated published product reviews attributed to authors who appeared not to exist.
- Reported provenance evidence includes author headshots traced to AI-generated image marketplaces and bios that disappeared after Futurism contacted the Arena Group.
- The article says content was supplied by AdVon Commerce and that the Arena Group denied the articles themselves were AI-generated while acknowledging licensed third-party content.
